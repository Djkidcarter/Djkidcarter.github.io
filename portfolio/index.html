Kaisa Oksanen Make-up


I am Kaisa. I got my makeup and hairstylist certification in 2006 in Finland from TI makeup centre. I have been working in the makeup and hair scene in many be different assignments in Finland and mainly in the UK for the past 10 years. I have worked as a theatre makeup artist, hairstylist and a makeup and hair designer for the Rujo theatre productions.
I have also worked as a wedding makeup and hair stylist, mainly in the UK with the lovely Jenna West makeup. We have covered multiple weddings together and I love making people feel beautiful in their own skin, enhancing the beautiful features they have. My trademark makeup is natural makeup, but I can also do glam.
With theatre makeup and hair love to throw myself into the artistic world and help create the best outcome with the director and the rest of the production team!
